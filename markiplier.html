ABOUT MARKIPLIER

Mark Edward Fischbach, better known as Markiplier, is an American YouTuber, actor, and comedian. He is best known for his Let's Play videos, vlogs, and comedic sketches.

Born on June 28, 1989, in Honolulu, Hawaii, Markiplier began his YouTube career in 2012. His channel quickly grew due to his energetic and humorous commentary, particularly on horror games like Five Nights at Freddy's.

With over 34 million subscribers and billions of views, Markiplier is one of the most popular gaming YouTubers. Beyond gaming, he's involved in charity work, voice acting, and has even created his own games.

CHARITY WORK

Giving Back to the Community

Markiplier is known for his extensive charity work, having raised millions of dollars for various causes through livestreams and special events.

Some of the organizations he's supported include the Cancer Research Institute, Save the Children, and the Cincinnati Children's Hospital.

His annual charity livestreams often feature special guests, challenges, and incentives to encourage donations from his fanbase.
